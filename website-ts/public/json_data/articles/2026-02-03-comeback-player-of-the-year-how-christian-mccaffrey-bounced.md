When he is healthy, the entire unit becomes harder to predict and harder to survive across four quarters.

McCaffrey’s 2024 was defined by interruptions, an injury-plagued season that prevented him from sustaining the kind of week-to-week rhythm that has long made him such a unique threat. His return “to form,” as ESPN noted, was not simply a matter of suiting up. It was a full reset: the vision between the tackles, the burst to turn a crease into a chunk gain, and the versatility that allows him to function as a runner, receiver and mismatch creator all at once. For an offense designed around timing and precision, that kind of dependable availability can be as valuable as any single explosive play.

For readers less familiar with McCaffrey’s role, the 49ers have leaned on him as a do-everything centerpiece since acquiring him to complement a system predicated on play-action, motion and yards after contact. That leverage is why his health has become a storyline that extends beyond injury reports.

McCaffrey’s bounce-back also matters because the postseason is less forgiving than the regular season.
