For Fields, declining the invitation — as reported — placed him squarely in the middle of that conversation. The Pro Bowl label can carry weight in contract language, legacy discussions and public perception, but the week itself has increasingly become optional in practice, particularly for players managing offseason priorities, health concerns or personal schedules.
